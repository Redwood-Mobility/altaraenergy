Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Altara Energy

About Altara Energy

What is Altara Energy?

Altara Energy is a city convenience EV charging network designed for daily drivers, fleet operators, and commuters. We provide fast, reliable DC charging at convenient city locations with 10+ high-power chargers at every station. Our focus is on efficient, dependable charging where you need it, when you need it.

Where are Altara stations located?

We're launching in major cities across the United States, starting with high-traffic metropolitan areas. Check our Locations page for current and upcoming stations. Sign up for notifications to be the first to know when we open in your area.

How is Altara different from other charging networks?

Altara focuses on high-capacity city charging with 10+ DC fast chargers per location (not just 4-6 like many competitors). We offer flat-rate pricing for Pathfinder Rewards members, eliminating time-of-use volatility. Our stations feature site-specific amenities that vary by location, adapting to each property's unique offerings rather than enforcing a one-size-fits-all approach.

Pathfinder Rewards

What is Pathfinder Rewards?

Pathfinder Rewards is a multi-network EV charging loyalty program. Think of it like airline alliances—one membership works across multiple independent charging networks. Join for free and enjoy benefits at Altara and other participating charging locations. Membership includes flat-rate pricing, app-based payments, real-time station availability, and usage tracking.

Where can I use my Pathfinder Rewards membership?

Your Pathfinder Rewards membership works at all Altara Energy locations and at other participating charging networks within the Pathfinder Network. The network is growing, with more charging operators joining regularly. Check the Pathfinder app for a complete list of participating locations.

What are the Pathfinder Rewards membership tiers?

Pathfinder Rewards offers four membership tiers:

  • Explorer (Free): Base flat-rate pricing, no time-of-use charges
  • Voyager: 5% credits back on all charging sessions
  • Pioneer: 10% credits back + $100 annual credit
  • Trailblazer (Invite-only): 15% credits back + $500 annual credit

Visit our Membership page for detailed pricing and benefits.

How do I join Pathfinder Rewards?

Joining is easy and free! Visit our Membership page to sign up for the Explorer tier at no cost. You can upgrade to Voyager or Pioneer at any time for additional benefits. Download the Pathfinder app to manage your account and find charging locations.

Charging & Vehicles

What vehicles can charge at Altara stations?

All current electric vehicles with DC fast charging capability can charge at Altara stations. Our chargers support both CCS (Combined Charging System) and NACS (North American Charging Standard, also known as Tesla connector) standards. This includes vehicles from Tesla, Ford, GM, Rivian, Hyundai, Kia, Volkswagen, BMW, Mercedes, and all other major EV manufacturers.

How fast is the charging?

Altara stations feature high-power DC fast chargers ranging from 150kW to 350kW. Actual charging speed depends on your vehicle's capabilities and battery state. Most modern EVs can add 100+ miles of range in 15-20 minutes. Your vehicle will automatically charge at the fastest safe rate it supports.

Do I need to bring my own charging cable?

No! All Altara DC fast chargers have built-in charging cables with the appropriate connectors. Simply pull up, plug in your vehicle, and authorize the session through the app or payment terminal.

Payment & Pricing

How do I pay for charging?

Pathfinder Rewards members can pay seamlessly through the mobile app. Simply start a charging session in the app, and payment is automatic using your saved payment method. Non-members can pay at the charging station using a credit card at the payment terminal, though they will be charged time-of-use rates rather than the flat member rates.

What does "flat member rate" mean?

Pathfinder Rewards members pay the same rate per kWh regardless of when they charge—no peak, mid-peak, or off-peak pricing. You pay the same predictable rate whether you charge at 3pm or 3am. Non-members pay time-of-use rates that vary by time of day, which can be significantly higher during peak hours.

Are there idle fees?

Yes, to ensure chargers remain available for all customers, we charge an idle fee if your vehicle remains plugged in after charging is complete and other customers are waiting. You'll receive a notification in the app when your charge session completes so you can move your vehicle promptly.

Amenities & Experience

What amenities are available at Altara stations?

Amenities vary by location since each Altara station partners with existing businesses and properties. Common amenities include clean restrooms, comfortable waiting areas, and nearby retail or food service. Some locations may offer shaded seating, picnic areas, dog parks, or other site-specific features. Check the station details in the app to see what's available at each location.

Is WiFi available?

WiFi availability depends on the specific location and site host. Many of our partner locations offer free WiFi to customers. Check the station details in the Pathfinder app for WiFi information at each location.

Are Altara stations open 24/7?

Most Altara charging stations are accessible 24/7 for charging, though amenities like restrooms or retail may have limited hours depending on the site host's business hours. The Pathfinder app shows operating hours for both charging and amenities at each location.
