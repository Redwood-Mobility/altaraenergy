Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Altara Energy

Altara Energy is a city convenience EV charging network designed for daily drivers. We provide fast, reliable DC charging at convenient city locations with 10+ high-power chargers at each station. Altara is part of the Pathfinder Rewards network, offering consistent flat-rate pricing and straightforward charging solutions for your daily drive.

Pathfinder Rewards is a multi-network loyalty program that works at Altara and partner charging networks. It's free to join and offers several membership tiers:

  • Explorer (Free): Flat-rate pricing with no time-of-use surprises
  • Voyager: 5% credits back on charging
  • Pioneer: 10% credits back + $100 annual credit
  • Trailblazer (Invite-only): 15% credits back + $500 annual credit

Your Pathfinder Rewards account works across all participating charging networks, giving you more charging options and more value.

Altara stations feature DC fast chargers compatible with all modern electric vehicles including Tesla (with adapter), Ford, GM, Rivian, Hyundai, Kia, Nissan, and more. Our chargers support both CCS and NACS (North American Charging Standard) connectors. If your vehicle can DC fast charge, it can charge at Altara.

Payment is simple and contactless through the Altara mobile app:

  1. Download the Altara app and create a free account
  2. Add a payment method (credit card, debit card, or digital wallet)
  3. Start charging by scanning the QR code on the charger or selecting it in the app
  4. Your payment is processed automatically when charging completes

All receipts and charging history are available in the app. Pathfinder Rewards members get flat-rate pricing with no time-of-use fluctuations.

Check the Altara app for specific amenities available at each location.
