Our Company

About Altara Energy

Our Mission

Altara Energy is building EV charging infrastructure where drivers need it most. We take a long-term ownership approach to charging infrastructure, investing in sites that serve daily drivers, fleet operators, and commercial customers.

Unlike networks that rely solely on site host capital or grant funding, we invest directly in charging infrastructure and operate every site we develop. This ownership model ensures consistent quality, reliable operations, and long-term commitment to every location.

Our Approach

Own and Operate: We invest in and maintain our charging infrastructure. No passing costs to site hosts or relying on third-party operators.

Partner for Success: We work with property owners, retailers, and municipalities to find the right sites and create mutually beneficial partnerships.

Deliver Reliability: Professional network operations, proactive maintenance, and 24/7 monitoring ensure drivers can count on our stations.
