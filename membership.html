Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pathfinder Rewards?

Pathfinder Rewards is a multi-network loyalty program that works at Altara Energy and partner charging networks. One account, multiple networks, maximum convenience.

Is there a free membership tier?

Yes! The Explorer tier is completely free with no credit card required. You'll get member pricing, earn Pathfinder Points, and enjoy flat-rate pricing with no time-of-use surprises.

What does "flat-rate pricing" mean?

Flat-rate means one consistent price any time of day. No time-of-use pricing, no surge charges. Charge at 3pm or 3am—same predictable rate.

When can I use my membership?

You can sign up for Pathfinder Rewards now and be ready when Altara stations open in 2026. Your membership will work at Altara and participating partner networks.

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Absolutely! Start with Explorer for free, then upgrade to Voyager, Pioneer, or Trailblazer tiers when they launch to unlock higher discounts and faster points earning.
